WOD #2

4 stations with 30-second rest to rotate between stations

 * 1 Rep Max Full Squat Snatch in 3 min (Scaled can do a power
snatch to OHS)

 * Weighted Pullup in 2 min (Scaled is # of pullups

 * Pistols—alternating legs in 1 min (Scaled is # of air squats

 * Handstand Pushups (kipping allowed) in 1 min (Scaled is # of situps

SCORE is total pounds plus total reps

Note: If you cannot do a movement you get a zero for that station, but your
score is the total pounds and reps added together. So, if you are Rx’d
but cannot do a handstand pushup, you would get a zero for that
station and your score would be your pounds on the snatch plus your
pounds on the weighted pullup, plus your pistol reps. If you are
scaled and cannot do pullups, you would get a zero on that station.
If you choose to modify, you could do jumping pullups but would then
not be eligible for a prize in the scaled division.
